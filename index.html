Research

As a Ph.D. student specializing in Applied Cryptography, my research primarily focuses on developing hash-based post-quantum secure digital signatures for next-generation IoT systems, such as Smart Grids. In addition to this work, I eagerly investigate privacy-enhancing technologies, like Multi-party Computation (MPC) and Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE), to address challenges related to privacy, decentralization, and transparency in distributed authentication systems and privacy-preserving artificial intelligence systems.
