Kiarash Sedghi

Post-Quantum & Threshold Cryptography

I am a Ph.D. researcher at the University of South Florida working at the intersection of post-quantum cryptography and secure multi-party computation. My research focuses on the design of threshold lattice-based digital signatures — including the NIST PQC standards ML-DSA and Falcon — with an emphasis on round complexity, communication efficiency, and signature compactness for scalable, quantum-resistant protocols.
